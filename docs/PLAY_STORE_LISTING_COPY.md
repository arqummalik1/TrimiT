## Full description (paste as-is, then tweak city names if needed)

```text
TrimiT is the salon booking app that connects customers with hair salons, beauty parlours, barber shops, and spa services — and gives salon owners simple appointment management in one place.

Whether you need a haircut, facial, manicure, or beard trim, discover salons near you, pick a service, choose an open time slot, and confirm your appointment in minutes. Salon owners use the same app to list services, set hours, accept bookings, and manage their day.

FOR CUSTOMERS — BOOK SALON APPOINTMENTS
• Find nearby salons on the map or list
• Browse services, prices, and reviews
• Book salon appointments with real-time availability
• Reschedule or manage upcoming visits
• Pay at the salon (cash) — simple and clear
• Push reminders so you never miss a booking

FOR SALON OWNERS — SALON MANAGEMENT MADE SIMPLE
• Create your salon profile and service menu
• Control opening hours and slot capacity
• Accept, complete, or manage customer bookings
• Dashboard for today’s appointments
• Notifications when a new booking arrives
• Upload photos so clients choose you with confidence

WHY TRIMIT
TrimiT is built for India’s salon and beauty industry: barbers, unisex salons, ladies’ parlours, and men’s grooming. One app for salon booking and salon business management — no separate tools for customers and staff.

Download TrimiT today:
• Customers — book your next salon visit
• Salon owners — grow bookings and organize your schedule

Questions? Visit https://trimi-t.vercel.app/contact or email admin@audentix.com
```
